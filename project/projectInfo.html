4-H Projects

There can be several types of 4-H projects. It doesn't always focus on just animals, which people might not be aware of. While my club does focus on animals there are many that don't and your local 4-H office can help you find the right club and project for you and your child. These are just a few examples, there are so many projects you can do with 4-H.
